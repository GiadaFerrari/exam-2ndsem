Energia Potenziale (2015)

23cmx56cmx1m

description:

a small leather purse encased in a glass box, a briefcase encased in a metal box and a luggage free of boundaries but at the lowest height.

concept:

‘Effimero Permanente’ (‘Permanent effimerity’) derives from a deeper understanding and reflection of the theme of flight. By giving up the figurative representation of flight the artist explore a vincolated suspension, a suspended moment in time where the magnet create the illusion of flying and moving toward each other, but just at the condition that they don’t move or separate from their current position. Their flight, symbol of freedom, is then reborn as a sort of slavery and prison.
